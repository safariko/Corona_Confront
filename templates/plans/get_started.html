Perfect Ratio


We aim for a ratio of 150 non-hospitalized to 1 hospitalized member.

If we reach the ratio, the deductible for 1 hospitalized person is fully reimbursed from 150 contributions.

If we don't reach the ratio, partial reimbursement will still help hospitalized members.
